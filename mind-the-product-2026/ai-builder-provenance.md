# AI Builder Provenance

LaunchProof was built during the hackathon submission window with Codex and GPT-5 as the AI builder workflow.

## What AI helped build

- Parsed the hackathon rules into product, shippedness, and Devpost requirements.
- Shaped the product concept around launch readiness for AI builders instead of another generic idea generator.
- Implemented the Next.js/React public app, including the brief, flows, risks, resilience, evidence, and pitch views.
- Wrote verification scripts for local UI coverage, public deployment checks, Pages-aware builds, and final submit readiness.
- Iterated the Devpost materials, gallery screenshots, demo-video script, and submission checklist.

## What the product does not claim

- LaunchProof is not presented as an autonomous runtime AI agent.
- The app does not claim guaranteed product-market fit or completed customer validation.
- Novus/Pendo evidence is framed as product-behavior instrumentation and dashboard proof, not as a substitute for real users.
